# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project is open to everyone. We are committed to a welcoming and respectful environment regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Be:**
- Respectful and constructive in discussions
- Open to different approaches and ideas
- Honest about limitations and bugs

**Don't:**
- Harass or demean other contributors
- Use AURA for harmful automations
- Share other people's private information

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the project maintainer. Accounts that repeatedly violate this policy will be blocked from the repository.
